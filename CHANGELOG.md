## [0.1.5] - 2026-2-2 "0.8.0 Patch + optimizations, ect."

- Fred and "Fuckers" now have Halloween costumes on the week 2 Fred mixes

- Updated backStageFred spotlight sprite to be extended
- Freds shoe is now positioned correctly in his miss sprites
- PhillyTrainFred window lights are one image and now change via the stage script
